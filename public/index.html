Platform Features

⚡
HTTP Range Streaming
Seek to any point instantly. Nginx handles range requests for smooth playback without buffering the entire file.
🔒
Role-Based Access
Admin and client accounts separated. Admins manage the inventory. Clients stream content. Nginx enforces the rules.
🚀
Nginx Reverse Proxy
Nginx sits in front — rate limiting, caching, security headers, and zero-downtime reloads baked in.
📦
Self-Hosted
Runs entirely on your local network. No cloud. No subscriptions. Your data stays on your machine.
